SteadyPlan Story

Why it exists

I needed one place where accounts, debts, savings, investments, goals, contributions, and retirement plans were visible together. Not scattered across apps and spreadsheets, and not hidden behind another platform asking for all my data.

SteadyPlan is the tool I built to keep facing the numbers — and to keep long-term planning connected to everyday choices.

I use it personally to stay honest about progress and assumptions.

It is shared because self-hosting and open-source tools matter — systems you can inspect, repair, back up, and own.

Built and maintained as a one-person project, with AI-assisted development, testing, and careful iteration.
