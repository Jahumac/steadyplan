Product showcase

SteadyPlan is a planning and visibility tool. It is not a bank, broker, adviser, or trading platform. It is designed to help ordinary people see where they stand, keep monthly maintenance grounded, and make steadier long-term decisions.

Scenario estimates use your inputs and assumptions. They are not guarantees.

Why SteadyPlan exists

SteadyPlan started as a personal project after serious money stress and debt/IVA. The turning point was visibility: facing the numbers clearly instead of avoiding them.

It is shared because self-hosting and open-source tools matter — systems you can inspect, repair, back up, and own.
