Product showcase

SteadyPlan is a planning and visibility tool. It is not a bank, broker, adviser, or trading platform. It is designed for long-term thinking and practical clarity.

Projections are scenario estimates based on your inputs. No promises.

Why SteadyPlan exists

SteadyPlan started as a personal project after serious money stress and debt/IVA. The turning point was visibility: facing the numbers clearly instead of avoiding them.

It is shared because self-hosting and open-source tools matter — systems you can inspect, repair, back up, and own.
