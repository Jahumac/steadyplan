Long-term finance planning, self-hosted.

A self-hosted UK finance planning app for people who want long-term visibility without handing their data to a fintech.

SteadyPlan helps you model accounts, contributions, goals, pensions, debts and retirement scenarios while keeping your data on your own server. It is a planning dashboard and scenario tool — not a bank, broker, or adviser.

Who it’s for

Self-hosters who want a calmer, longer-term view than a spreadsheet — and who prefer to keep financial data on their own machine.

What it can model

Accounts and balances, monthly contributions, budgets, pensions, debts, ISA/LISA/pension allowances, goals, and retirement scenarios.

Why self-host it

Local SQLite database, no cloud sync. Optional external price lookups can be enabled, but the app works without them.

Data ownership and confidence

Your day-to-day data is stored in a local SQLite database. SteadyPlan supports broker CSV import, per-user JSON export/restore, and whole-instance SQLite backups.

What SteadyPlan is (and isn’t)

SteadyPlan is a tracking dashboard and scenario tool. It does not connect to your bank, and it does not provide financial advice. Projections are assumptions-based estimates.

Runs well on Docker/Unraid

The common setup is Docker or Unraid with a host-mounted data directory. You control where data is stored and how it’s backed up.
