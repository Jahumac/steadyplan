Product truth

Planning and visibility — on your own server

See where you stand

Bring accounts, debts, savings, investments and goals into one view you control.

Separate accessible vs locked money

Keep “available now” distinct from money reserved for later or tied to long-term plans.

Connect monthly choices to long-term scenarios

Use assumptions-based projections to explore trade-offs without pretending the future is guaranteed.

Keep control of your data

Local SQLite database on your own hardware. No cloud sync.

Back up and restore

Clear guidance for exports, backups, and what “restore” actually means.

Use it from any device

Run SteadyPlan on a server/NAS/desktop and access it from desktop, tablet, or phone browser. PWA where supported.

Founder

Built because facing the numbers matters

SteadyPlan started as a personal project after serious money stress and debt/IVA. Visibility was the turning point: one place to see the full picture without handing everything to another platform.

Shared as a self-hosted/open-source project for people who value local control, repairability, and backups.

What it is / what it is not

Clear boundaries, plain language

What it is

  • A self-hosted planning and visibility tool
  • Built for UK-focused long-term planning
  • Useful for accounts, debts, savings, investments, goals and retirement scenarios
  • Designed around calm clarity

What it is not

  • Not a bank
  • Not a broker
  • Not financial advice
  • Not a trading or get-rich-quick product
  • Not a paid hosted SaaS product yet
