SteadyPlan

Long-term finance planning, self-hosted.

Face the numbers. Keep control. Plan steadily. SteadyPlan helps you model accounts, debts, contributions, goals, investments and retirement scenarios in one private place — without handing your financial life to another platform.

Start with docs See how the tour could work
Core stance

Runs on your own server/NAS/desktop. Use it from desktop, tablet or phone browser. Install as a web app (PWA) where supported. No bank linking. No promises. Just visibility and planning.

Why it exists

SteadyPlan started as a personal project after serious money stress and debt/IVA. The turning point wasn’t a trick — it was visibility. I needed one clear place to see the full picture and stay engaged with it, without sending my entire financial life to another platform.

Shared as a self-hosted/open-source project for people who value local control, repairability and backups.

What SteadyPlan helps with

See the full picture

Accounts, debts, savings, investments and goals in one view you control.

Track accounts and holdings

Manual-first tracking with optional external price lookups (if you enable them).

Plan contributions and goals

Keep month-to-month inputs consistent so your plan stays grounded.

Understand retirement scenarios

Scenario estimates driven by your balances, contributions and assumptions.

Keep data local

Local SQLite database. No cloud sync.

Back up and restore

Your server, your backups — with clear restore boundaries.

Ways to use it

Run it

On your own server/NAS/desktop. Docker/Unraid friendly.

Use it

From desktop, tablet or phone browser on your LAN/VPN.

Install it

As a web app (PWA) where supported by your browser.

Public exposure is optional and should be configured intentionally (HTTPS + trusted reverse proxy).
