How the product helps

Where you are today

SteadyPlan is designed to make the current picture legible, so decisions are based on reality instead of avoidance.

Cash-accessible, invested-accessible, restricted, and locked-for-later money

Long-term planning is easier when you separate cash you could reach now, invested money you could sell, money with conditions, and money reserved for later.

Scenarios, not promises

Scenario estimates are assumptions-based tools to explore trade-offs, not a guarantee of outcomes.

Key flows today

Overview answers first

The app now starts by showing the current picture, what needs attention, and the next sensible action before deeper admin detail.

Monthly Update becomes financial truth

SteadyPlan uses a lightweight monthly check-in to confirm balances, contributions, and notes before those numbers feed allowances and performance.

Diagnostics keep trust visible

Settings keeps backup health, restore boundaries, assistant scope, and read-only broker review close to the product so trust is inspectable.

Scenario estimates

The scenario estimates view is built around assumptions you control. The goal is to support long-term thinking while staying clear about what is entered data and what is forecast output.

Estimates are based on your inputs and assumptions.

Data ownership

SteadyPlan stores your data locally in a SQLite database on your own server/NAS/desktop. There is no hosted account and no public signup, and the safest hands-on evaluation path is still your own local install.

Optional external price lookups can contact providers for ticker data. Everything else stays local unless you choose otherwise.
