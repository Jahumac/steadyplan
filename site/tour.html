Overview

See where you stand

A single place to view accounts, goals, and the current month without having to stitch the story together from multiple apps or spreadsheets.

Why it exists: visibility makes it easier to keep going, especially when things feel uncertain.

Accessible vs locked money

Separate what you can use now from what is for later

SteadyPlan is designed to avoid a single misleading “total”. It helps keep accessible money distinct from money that is locked, restricted, or part of longer retirement planning.

Why it exists: people make better decisions when the numbers match reality.

Monthly choices

Connect the month to the plan

Monthly contributions and reviews keep long-term scenarios anchored in what is actually happening, not what you hope is happening.

Why it exists: plans drift when the monthly picture is ignored.

Scenarios

Explore long-term scenarios, without promises

SteadyPlan uses assumptions and your inputs to generate scenario estimates. The purpose is exploration and planning, not certainty.

Why it exists: seeing “what changes if…” helps you make trade-offs with less guesswork.

Settings and data ownership

Local-first, explicit boundaries

Your data lives in a local SQLite database on your own hardware. There is no cloud sync.

Why it exists: self-hosting only feels trustworthy when you can inspect and back up the system.

Backups and restore

Back up with confidence

SteadyPlan supports per-user JSON exports and whole-instance SQLite backups. Restore boundaries are explained plainly.

Why it exists: a plan is only useful if you can recover your data after mistakes or updates.
