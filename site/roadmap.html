Roadmap

SteadyPlan is self-hosted today. The focus is clarity, safety, trust, and usability.

This roadmap is intentionally grounded. It describes what exists, what is improving next, and what is only being explored later.

Hosted SteadyPlan is being explored — not launched. No timeline yet. Only if data safety, supportability, and trust are good enough.

Available now

Self-hosted app

  • UK-focused accounts and long-term planning
  • Goals, budget, holdings, retirement scenarios
  • Privacy-first local data model (SQLite)
  • Backup/export/restore guidance

Improving next

Clarity and first-use

  • Clearer onboarding
  • Better mobile/PWA experience
  • Simpler first-use journey
  • Better demo/sample walkthrough
  • More polished public tour

Exploring later

Hosted SteadyPlan (only if it becomes supportable)

  • Invite-only private testing
  • No timeline yet
  • Only if data safety, supportability, and trust are good enough
