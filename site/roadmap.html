SteadyPlan

Roadmap

SteadyPlan is self-hosted today. The focus is clarity, safety, trust, and usability.

This roadmap describes direction, not promises. It shows what exists, what is improving next, and what is only being explored later.

Hosted SteadyPlan is being explored — not launched. No timeline yet.

Product direction Grounded
  • Clearer first-use and onboarding
  • Better mobile and PWA experience
  • More polished public tour and docs

Three horizons

Available now Self-hosted
  • UK-focused accounts and planning
  • Goals, holdings, and retirement scenarios
  • Privacy-first local data model (SQLite)
  • Backup/export/restore guidance
Improving next Clarity
  • Clearer onboarding
  • Simpler first-use journey
  • Better demo/sample walkthrough
  • More polished public tour
Exploring later No timeline
  • Hosted SteadyPlan (exploratory)
  • Invite-only private testing
  • Only if safety and supportability are good enough

Hosted SteadyPlan is only considered if it becomes clear, safe, trustworthy, and supportable.
