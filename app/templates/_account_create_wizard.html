{# ── Step 1 · The basics ────────────────────────────────────── #}

What's this account called?

Every penny needs a home. Give Shelly a name for this one, tell him who it's with and what kind of wrapper it sits in — he'll take it from there.

{# ── Step 2 · Balance & Growth ──────────────────────────────── #}

How is this account valued?

Shelly needs to know how to track what this account is worth. If it holds investments, he can calculate the value from your holdings. Otherwise just tell him the balance and he'll keep things tidy.

{# ── Advanced: Fees (collapsible, below the main grid) ────── #}
Advanced: Fees
All optional — Shelly combines annual fees into one effective rate. Contribution fee reduces each payment before it's invested.
{# ── Step 3 · Holdings (only for holdings accounts) ─────────── #}

What's in this account?

Tell Shelly what you're holding — search by ticker, or add funds manually if there's no ticker. Don't worry about getting every last one right now; you can always add more later.

Shelly checks live market data providers and prefers the London Stock Exchange listing. If the price looks wrong, try adding .L to the ticker (e.g. VWRP.L).
