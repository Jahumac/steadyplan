How this works

Monthly Update Guide

1
Wait for investments to settle

After your contributions go in{% if assumptions and assumptions.salary_day %} (around the {{ assumptions.salary_day }}{{ 'st' if assumptions.salary_day in [1,21,31] else ('nd' if assumptions.salary_day in [2,22] else ('rd' if assumptions.salary_day in [3,23] else 'th')) }}){% endif %}, allow a couple of business days for settlement. {% if review_ready_date %} This month you're good to update from {{ review_ready_date.strftime('%-d %B') }}.{% endif %}

2
Update your holdings

For each holdings-based account, update the units and price for every fund or ETF. You can type them in directly or use the ↻ button to fetch live prices.

3
Update manual accounts

For savings accounts, workplace pensions, or anything without individual holdings — just enter the total balance shown by your provider.

4
Mark as complete

Once everything's up to date, hit "Mark as Complete" and Shelly will snapshot your net worth for the month. Any one-off top-ups or bonuses? Log them on the Allowances page so your tax-year totals stay accurate.
