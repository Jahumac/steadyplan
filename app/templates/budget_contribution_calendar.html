Fast setup

Yearly pot-fill pattern

Use this for things like Lifetime ISA: “put £1,000 in for 4 months every year”. It creates one yearly block per year, instead of making you type every month by hand.

Example: £1,000 × 4 months each year fills a £4,000 LISA allowance. For an uneven run, enter comma amounts like 1500, 1500, 1000.
