How to read Performance

Opening / Imported is money already there when tracking started. It is not treated as a contribution or gain.

Contributed is money added or withdrawn after tracking started, including transfers recorded as cash-flow events.

Gain / Interest is what changed after those flows. Cash ISA uses cash interest; Premium Bonds use prize gain; investment accounts use market gain/loss.

Annualised appears only after enough history, so early months do not look artificially dramatic.
